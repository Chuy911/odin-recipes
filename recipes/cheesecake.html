Cheesecake

Description

A tasty and easy to make cheesecake to die for.

Ingredients

Crust Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of graham cracker crumbs or 9 to 10 graham crackers crushed
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 5 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Filling Ingredients

  • Two 8 ounce packages of cream cheese room temperature
  • 2/3 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and line a 9 inch spring form pan with butter
  2. Stir all crust ingredients together until fully combined
  3. Fill bottom of pan with crust crumbs and form a layer by pressing down the crumbs
  4. Mix the cream cheese and sugar until smooth
  5. Then mix eggs and vanilla slowly until just incorporated and smooth
  6. Pour filling into the pan and place inside of oven for 30 minutes
  7. Remove pan from oven and allow to cool until safe to transfer into fridge for additional 12 hrs
  8. Add any toppings if you'd like and enjoy
