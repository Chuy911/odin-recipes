Brownies

Description

Delectable mouthwatering brownies that explode chocolaty goodness inside your mouth.

Ingredients
  • 1 1/4 cups of sugar
  • 3/4 cups of butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspons vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 8 ounces dark chocolate

Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Melt half of the chocolate into chuncks and save rest
  3. Mix butter and sugar until homogenous then mix eggs until fluffy
  4. Mix vanilla extract, salt and melted chocolate into mixture
  5. Add the dry ingredients and fold into batter until smooth and homogenous
  6. Chop other half of chocolate and fold into the batter
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes then let cool completely
  8. Enjoy!
